iTana is a Swahili word which means reach out. We are a community-based organization that seeks to address mental health through, film, podcasts, commmunit dialogues, outreaches and social hangouts.

iTana uses these mediums as they are the easy platforms in reach and also the most appreciated ways of message receivership. iTana sessions are dubbed Behind Closed Hearts which is a forum where people of all gender and generations meet to discuss, dissect and direct conversations to meet the right avenue of thought and action.

this is forum meant to build a strong local community through promotion and creation of awareness on the importance of mental health.
